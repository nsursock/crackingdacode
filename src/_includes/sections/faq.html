Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.

Were you really a European secret agent in the past?
Yes I was. I participated in the War on Terror since 2001 and infiltrated Ben Laden's network until 2011 when I called it quits.
Why do you call yourself a modern Donnie Brasco?
That's because my mission was successful but the government never paid me. They owe me 25 million dollars. I had to rely on my savings and my skills as a webdev and a musician.
Is blogging your only occupation?
No, of course not. I'm writing a novel about my experience that should be out in 6 months if everything goes well. I also love music and was contacted by a record label to produce some of my tracks. .
Why can't I comment on your posts?
The comment system will soon be available. Please wait a few months. In the meantime, you can send me a message through the contact page.
How do I leave a testimonial?
On the contact page, use the subject field and specify "Testimonial". Testimonials will be featured randomly around the website. After receiving your message, you might be asked for a picture to display next to the testimonial.
